Ho Chi Minh City Apartment Heatmap

About This Project

For this project, I wanted to map out the distribution of real estate prices across Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I wrote a Python script to scrape the pricing on batdongsan.com and used pandas to clean and process the dataset.

To visualize the findings, I utilized Plotly to create an interactive scatter map. The size and color of the markers correspond to the price per square meter, making it easy to spot the most expensive districts and pricing trends at a glance.
