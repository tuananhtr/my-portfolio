I am Andy Trinh, a Business Consultant and Data Analyst.

My professional journey spans seven years in Business Consulting and Data Analytics, primarily within the Fintech and E-commerce sectors. I have worked on translating complex datasets into actionable strategies for high-growth environments. Navigating these fast-paced industries taught me how to effectively bridge the gap between technical analysis and business goals, ensuring that I use data science to solve real-world problems rather than just theoretical ones.

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Statistics and Data Science at KU Leuven. I love the rigorous statistical methods I am learning in the program and applying them to the practical challenges I faced in my career. This combination of industry experience and academic depth allows me to approach data problems with both agility and precision.

I believe in maintaining a healthy balance between mental rigor and physical activity. I love playing badminton and running, which help me clear my mind after diving deep into data. Music is my creative outlet, and I love playing guitar whenever I have free time to unwind and find a different kind of rhythm.
